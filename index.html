Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is WiseSkills for?

Nigerian secondary school students and WAEC/JAMB graduates who want a data-driven, personalised guide to choosing the right university course and career path.

How accurate is the matching?

Our algorithm analyses over 80 variables including your WAEC grades, JAMB scores, hobbies, and personality profile. Users report a 94% satisfaction rate with their matched career paths.

What if I have a low JAMB score?

A low JAMB score doesn't close every door. WiseSkills identifies polytechnic routes, direct entry options, and lower cut-off universities that still offer strong career outcomes.

Do I need my parents' permission?

Not at all — this is your journey. However, the Elite Mentorship plan includes a parent-inclusive career briefing session if you'd like to get your family on board.

I don't know what I enjoy — can this still help?

Absolutely. Our interest discovery quiz is designed to help you identify what you enjoy even if you've never thought about it formally. Many students discover new passions through the process.

Is WiseSkills affiliated with JAMB or WAEC?

No, WiseSkills is an independent career guidance platform. Our recommendations are unbiased and based solely on your individual profile.
